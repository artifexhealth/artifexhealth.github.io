Our mission

We build health software that makes personal data easier to understand, more useful in daily life, and more actionable for improving sleep, recovery, performance, and activity trends.

With a background in gaming, and a love for health and wellness, we design with passion and intention. Seeing your own metrics is great, but we want you to do more than just look, we want you to act. Our goal is to make users aware of their metrics, have context behind them, and have a personally curated solution to improving them. We want all of this to happen while making it fun to be healthy. We've successfully built that with Artifex Health. Creating a seamless in-app experience, where the user can earn achievements, track their metrics, and have a personal AI assistant that nudges them to create good habits and trends, all while giving them personal insight into their daily health and wellness.

Featured app

Artifex Health is designed to transform raw wearable and phone data into meaningful feedback. Instead of overwhelming users with disconnected metrics, Artifex focuses on context, clarity, and insightful analysis. Helping users understand what changed, why it matters, and what to do next. With the use of AI, personal tags, and sensory data, users get personalized coaching insights in the palm of their hand.

  • Sleep stage and sleep quality insights
  • Recovery and daily readiness scoring
  • Heart rate and resting trend analysis
  • Daily Calorie Burn Tracking
  • Personalized Target Coaching

Key product highlights

Sleep Intelligence

Give yourself better context around your nightly sleep structure, duration, and recovery quality so you can understand what happened while you were sleeping.

Readiness Scoring

Show a concise daily signal that helps users decide whether to push, maintain, or recover based on overnight recovery and previous exertion.
